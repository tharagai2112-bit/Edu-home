F. A. Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've answered the most common ones below to help you get started quickly and confidently.

Where can I access study materials after enrollment?

Once enrolled, all study materials-including notes, videos and mock tests-will be available in your personalized dashboard. You can access them anytime, anywhere.

Are the classes live or recorded?

We provide a mix of both. You get access to live interactive sessions for doubt clearing and concept building, along with high-quality recorded lectures for flexible learning.

Do you provide mentorship or doubt-clearing sessions?

Yes, absolutely. Every student is assigned a dedicated mentor who tracks progress. We also conduct weekly live doubt-clearing sessions with expert faculties.

Can I switch courses or upgrade after enrollment?

Yes, upgrades are possible within the first week of enrollment. Please contact our support team for assistance with course transitions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and EMI options are also available for select courses.
